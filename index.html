About this site

This is a static HTML/CSS site with a Node.js generator to produce per-card pages from RoyaleAPI. Run node scripts/generate_cards.js with your ROYALEAPI_TOKEN to regenerate cards. The design emphasizes readability, contrast, and quick navigation.
